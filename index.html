What is it for?

Application in real estate

Residential complex maps

Detailed maps of residential complexes showcasing building layouts, amenities, and surrounding areas.

Helps potential buyers visualize the entire complex and understand the distribution of facilities.

Floor plans

Precise floor plans for individual units or entire buildings.

Aids in space planning, interior design, and providing a clear view of unit layouts for potential buyers.

Infrastructure placement maps

Maps highlighting the location of nearby infrastructure such as schools, hospitals, parks, and shopping centers.

Assists buyers in evaluating the convenience and accessibility of essential services and amenities.

Property boundary maps

Detailed maps showing the exact boundaries of properties.

Essential for legal documentation, resolving disputes, and ensuring accurate property transactions.

Development site maps

Maps outlining proposed developments and construction sites.

Useful for urban planning, investor presentations, and public consultations.

Virtual tours with interactive maps

Interactive maps integrated with virtual tours of properties.

Enhances the online viewing experience, allowing potential buyers to explore properties remotely.

Market analysis maps

Maps showing market trends, property values, and demographic information.

Helps investors and developers make informed decisions based on comprehensive market data.

Environmental impact maps

Maps assessing the environmental impact of new developments.

Crucial for ensuring sustainable development and compliance with environmental regulations.

Historical property maps

Maps showing historical data and previous developments in the area.

Useful for heritage conservation, understanding development patterns, and historical research.

Residential Sales Project - UAE

Building model on Palm Jumeirah, Dubai featuring residence statuses: Free, Rented, or Sold. The 57 floors offer navigation and details, including building, floor, and unit status; number of bedrooms; suite area; balcony area; and total plan. These case studies are designed to provide an in-depth look at the building's features and unit availability, enabling potential buyers and renters to make informed decisions.

Land And Property Management App for Brokers - USA

The advanced land and property management tool that leverages the latest technological possibilities for real estate brokerage, featuring advanced mapping, custom layers, detailed parcel data, and many other essential features. It provides effortless access to aerial views, sales history, property appraisal links, and other critical features for effective project management.

Technologies:

Facility and Asset Management Solution - USA 

The Management Tool is designed optimize operations. This platform allows users to select specific dates, floors, and times to view and manage facility data with ease. With a color legend bar, users can instantly interpret various values and metrics, enabling informed efficient management. This tool is a game-changer for operators, ensuring smooth operations and an enhanced experience for owners.

3D Vizualization and Arrangement Tool - USA

The interactive 3D space model facilitates the movement and seamless arrangement of furniture, décor elements in the rooms, providing a versatile and immersive design experience. Fuse GIS Toolkit: Spatial Registry for Texas Counties - USA

Registry Software for Austin, Dallas, Houston, and San Antonio counties, Texas. This solution offers an additional set of spatial layers for flexible searches of up-to-date location information. It features detailed maps with various vector layers for zoning, including affordable housing, permits, floodplains, and transportation. Users can overlay multiple data layers for comprehensive insights.
